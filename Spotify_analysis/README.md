# Spotify Data Analysis

## Overview
This analysis aims to analyze Spotify data to derive insights and address various business problems related to music trends, user preferences.

## Objectives
- Address business questions related to artist popularity, song characteristics, and user behavior.

## Business Questions
- What are the trends in song popularity over time?
- How do various features of songs (like danceability, energy) correlate with their popularity?
- What genres are most popular among different demographics?
- How does the release of a new album affect the popularity of its tracks?

## Requirements
- SQL (if querying data)
